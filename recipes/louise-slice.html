Louise Slice

Description

This is a very tasty classic slice with a biscuit base, a jam filling and a meringue topping. It takes 1 hour to make and produces 24 pieces

Ingredients

Base

  • 75g Butter (softened)
  • 1/4 cup White Sugar
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 1/4 cups Plain Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder

Topping

  • 1/4 cup Raspberry, Plum or Blackcurrant Jam
  • 2 Egg Whites
  • 1/2 cup Caster Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Dessicated Coconut

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C. Lightly grease a 20cm x 30cm shallow tin and line with baking paper.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy, then add egg yolks and mix thoroughly.
  3. Add the lemon juice, then sift the flour and baking powder and mix to form a firm dough.
  4. Press the dough evenly into the prepared tin and spread over the jam. You don't need a thick layer.
  5. Beat the egg whites until stiff then gently fold in the caster sugar and the coconut using a metal spoon.
  6. Spread the egg white mixture carefully over the jam, trying to keep an even thickness. Sprinkle with a little more coconut.
  7. Bake for about 25 minutes until the coconut is just turning golden brown.
  8. Cut into squares or fingers while it is still warm. Cool in the tin on a wire rack.
