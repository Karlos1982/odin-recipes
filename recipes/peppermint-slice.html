Peppermint Slice

Description

This is a delicious Peppermint Slice that is easy to make and produces about 20 pieces.

Ingredients

Base

  • 115g Butter
  • 1/2 cup White Sugar
  • 1 cup Plain Flour
  • 1/2 cup Dessicated Coconut
  • 1/4 cup Cocoa
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder

Peppermint Filling

  • 3 cups Icing Sugar
  • 30g Butter
  • 3 tbsp Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Peppermint Essence

Chocolate Topping

  • 200g Chocolate (Milk or Dark)
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Method

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 180C. Line and grease a 20cm square tin.
  2. In a medium sized bowl add butter and sugar. Cream together for 3-4 minutes or until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the dry ingredients (flour, cocoa, coconut and baking powder) to the bowl with the creamed mixture.
  4. Fold the dry ingredients into the creamed mixture until well combined.
  5. Use the back of a spoon to press mixture into the prepared tin.
  6. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes.

Peppermint Filling

While the base is baking in the oven, make the peppermint filling.

  1. Add the icing sugar, butter, milk and peppermint essence to a medium sized bowl.
  2. Fold together until well combined with a wooden spoon. It should be pretty thick.
  3. While the base is still hot, add the peppermint filling on top and spread it out evenly, making sure to get into the corners.
  4. Leave to cool for about 30-60 minutes before making the topping.

Chocolate Topping

Once the base and filling has cooled make the chocolate topping.

  1. Break or cut the chocolate up into small pieces and put into a heat proof bowl.
  2. Pour the oil over the top of the chocolate and melt in a microwave for 30 second bursts, stirring between bursts.
  3. Pour the melted chocolate over the cooled peppermint filling and tilt the pan to spread the chocolate evenly.
  4. Put the slice in the fridge for 2-3 hours or overnight to set.
  5. Once set, cut slice into squares and enjoy.
